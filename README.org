* Welcome

The purpose of this workshop is to gather researchers of REDDIE WP4
who are interested in emulating a diabetes target trial in register
data. Specifically, we will work with (a modified copy of) the
R-package =ltmle=.

The meeting is organized as a mixture of 20 minute talks, a lot of
time for discussion, and hands-on programming in R.
